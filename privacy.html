Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to SANGHX. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your Name, Mobile Number, and Delivery Address to fulfill your orders. We do not store sensitive payment information on our servers.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely for:

• Processing and delivering orders.
• Contacting you regarding order status.
• Improving our app experience.

3. Data Sharing

We share your address and phone number only with the Delivery Partner assigned to your order. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your account information.
