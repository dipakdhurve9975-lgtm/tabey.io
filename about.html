About TABEY

Who We Are

TABEY is more than just a food delivery app. We are a community-driven platform dedicated to connecting hungry foodies with the best local restaurants, cafes, and home kitchens in the city. Born out of a passion for great food and technology, TABEY aims to revolutionize hyper-local delivery.

Our Vision

We envision a world where craving your favorite meal doesn't mean waiting for hours. We are building a logistics network that prioritizes speed, hygiene, and customer satisfaction above all else.
